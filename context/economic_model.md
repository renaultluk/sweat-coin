## Executive Summary

FitDAO creates a circular health economy where:

- **Users** earn SWEAT tokens by being healthy (soft-pegged to $1)
- **Researchers** pay ETH to access aggregated health data (reusable datasets!)
- **Merchants** offer coupons and receive generous ETH+SWEAT subsidies
- **Treasury** collects ETH from premium features and data marketplace
- **Platform** creates sustainable economic cycle with price stability

**Key Innovations:**

1. **Reusable Data Marketplace:** Same dataset sold to multiple researchers = massive revenue scaling
2. **Soft Peg Mechanism:** SWEAT maintained at $1 (Â±20%) for predictability
3. **Merchant Marketing Value:** Generous subsidies justified by customer acquisition benefits
4. **Dual Revenue Streams:** Premium features + data marketplace fund sustainable ecosystem
